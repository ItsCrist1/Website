About This Laptop

The Dell Vostro 3520 is a business-aimed versatile laptop designed for efficient and professional use, meaning it can perform decently in various tasks such as creative work, gaming (despite the lack of a dedicated graphics card) and programming or software development.

  • Office: Runs anything Office related smoothly.
  • Content Creation: Handles photo and video editing software effectively at 1080p.
  • Programming: Can run any IDE efficiently and most game engines for 2D or light 3D game development.
  • Gaming: Can run most indie games and some older AAA titles on lower settings at its native resolution.
  • Media Consumption: Acceptable 4k and smooth 2k video playback.
